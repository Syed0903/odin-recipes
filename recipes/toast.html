Quick, easy meal you can make for breakfast:

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Avacodo

Steps:

  1. Toast bread in a toaster, or in a pan with butter
  2. Put some butter in a pan and crack some eggs
  3. Cook the eggs slowly and chop some avocado
  4. Apply avocado to bread
  5. Add some water in a pan and cover, to cook the top of the egg
  6. When the egg is cooked to your liking, season with salt and pepper put eggs on top of the toast and put hot sauce Enjoy
